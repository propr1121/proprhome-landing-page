About ProprHome

Building the future of real estate in Portugal

Our Mission

ProprHome was founded with a simple yet ambitious goal: to create a real estate platform where everyone wins. We believe that the traditional real estate model is broken, with information silos, misaligned incentives, and outdated technology creating friction for agents, agencies, and clients alike.

We're building something different. A platform that leverages AI, blockchain verification, and real-time data sharing to create transparency, efficiency, and mutual success for all participants in the property market.

Our Story

ProprHome emerged from firsthand experience with the frustrations of the Portuguese real estate market. Our founders, having navigated the complexities of buying, selling, and renting properties in Portugal, recognized the need for a modern, technology-driven approach.

What started as a vision to improve the property search experience has evolved into a comprehensive platform that serves the entire real estate ecosystem. Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of real estate innovation in Portugal.

Our Values

Trust & Transparency

We believe in open, honest relationships. Our blockchain-verified listings and real-time data ensure everyone has access to accurate information.

Collaboration

Success in real estate is a team effort. Our platform is designed to foster collaboration between agents, agencies, and clients.

Innovation

We're constantly pushing boundaries with AI-powered matching, smart analytics, and cutting-edge technology to improve the real estate experience.

Client First

Every decision we make starts with the question: "How does this benefit our users?" Our success is measured by your success.

Our Team

We're a diverse team of technologists, real estate professionals, and design experts united by our passion for transforming the property market. Based in Portugal, we combine local market expertise with global technology standards.
